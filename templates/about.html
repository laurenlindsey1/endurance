Endurance is a webapp that functions as an online fitness journal. With Endurance you can upload and store your customized workout routines for later access. If you do not wish to create your own routine you may use one of the routines Endurance provides.


In addition to storing routines, exercises suggested by Endurance link directly to short YouTube videos and playlists that will demonstrate how to perform exercises. After completing your workout routine, you can input the type of exercise you have done along with the quantity and Endurance will record this amount.


Once you have completed a routine, Endurance will award you a gif to keep you motivated.
